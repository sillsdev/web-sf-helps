Frequently Asked Questions

Can I... ?

  • Can I ask a question? Only people with the role of Administrator can add a question. People in other roles can add an answer or add a comment.

  • Open the project in more than one tab? Yes, on a desktop, Scripture Forge runs in a web browser. Browsers allow you to open a hyperlink in a new tab.

In Scripture Forge, to open an area (tool) in another tab, you can press the Ctrl key and then click an area in the Navigation pane. Alternately, you can click an area in the Navigation pane with the middle mouse button (which is often the scroll wheel). These also work with any hyperlinked text you see in the Scripture Forge UI, such as those in the Settings area.

  • Can I continue to work without Internet access ()? Yes, you can continue to work with some limitations.
    See Also: Open Scripture Forge with the desktop icon and Log in/Log out.

  • If you see an Audio unavailable message, click the question mark button () to learn why.

How do I... ?

  • How do I change roles? Paratext roles can only be changed in Paratext. If others were invited to check a translation, they are given the role of Community Checker.

What is Scripture Forge?

  • See Scripture Forge overview or https://scriptureforge.org/.

Who can... ?

  • Roles lists the roles used in Scripture Forge. Tasks overview lists tasks by role.

Why can't I... ?

  • Why can't I see a question? You cannot see an archived question or its answers or comments.

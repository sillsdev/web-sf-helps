Save

In Scripture Forge, there are times when you need to click a button to save your work. Examples: SAVE QUESTION, SAVE ANSWER and SAVE COMMENT.

Translated contents are saved automatically locally, and synchronized by people with the role of Administrator. This updates the Paratext repository.

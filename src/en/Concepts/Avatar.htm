Avatar

An avatar is a graphical image that represents the user. The image could be a picture of the user or any other picture that the user wants to use instead. Before you choose a particular picture, the avatar shows the person's initials.

Your avatar appears on the Top App Bar.

When you click your avatar, you see these commands:

  • Logged in as

You see your name and an edit pencil () so you can update your name.

  • Project home

It opens Scripture Forge to projects you can choose.

  • Log out.

Note

  • Online means your computer has Internet access. Offline means it does not have Internet access.

  • Your avatar also appears with any answers you added to questions.

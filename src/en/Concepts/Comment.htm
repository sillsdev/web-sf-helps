Comment

A comment is usually a statement (remark or observation) about an answer or another comment. For example, you may want to reply to another person's comments on your answer so they understand your perspective.

A comment could also include a clarifying question. For example, you may need to ask a question to gain understanding of another person's answer.

  • People with the role of Administrator can do these tasks:

    • Delete another person's comment.

  • People with other roles can do these tasks, if enabled:

    • Add a comment.

    • Delete your own comment.

    • Edit your own comment.

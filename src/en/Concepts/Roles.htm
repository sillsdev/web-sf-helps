Roles

Roles determine what a person can see or do in a project. Tasks overview has a table of roles and tasks.

Here is an overview of roles:

Roles from Paratext

The roles of Administrator, Translator, Reviewer and Observer come from Paratext and come into effect when they log in to Scripture Forge with their Paratext account. They follow the permissions that Paratext gives for these roles and can only be changed in Paratext.

  • Administrator (of a project) - Administrator tasks overview lists the things they can do.

  • Translator - Translate tasks overview lists the things they can do.

  • Reviewer - People with this role can only look at the translated texts, but can add answers to questions, work with comments and like answers.

  • Observer - People with this role can only look at the translated texts, questions, answers and likes.

Roles unique to Scripture Forge

These are Scripture Forge roles. Typically, people who are invited do not have existing roles in the Paratext project. So, they will have one of these roles.

  • Community Checker - Community Checking tasks overview lists the things they can do.

  • View Translation - People with this role can only look at the translated texts, questions, answers and likes.

Note

  • Share - if enabled, you can invite people to share in the task of community checking or to just look at translations. These people must accept their invitation to be activated with user credentials (password and so on).

  • Users has a filter so you can filter users by role. It also has the Share with others control.

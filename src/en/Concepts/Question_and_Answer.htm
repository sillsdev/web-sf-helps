Question and Answer

A question is an inquiry that is intended to get information or clarification from other people.

An answer is a reply to the question. See Also: Comments.

  • People with the role of Administrator can do these tasks:

    • Add questions to direct and control discussions about the translation work. Questions can be typed text and/or recorded audio.

    • Archive or republish a question.

    • Delete a question.

    • Delete another person's answer.

    • Edit a question.

    • Read or hear an answer.

  • People with other roles can do these tasks, if they are enabled:

    • Add an answer. Answers can be typed text and/or recorded audio.

Other peoples' answers are hidden until you add your own answer. This way your answer is not affected by other existing answers. If you see a link that says Show 1 or more answers (or similar), click it to see other answers.

    • Add a comment.

    • Delete your answer.

    • Edit your answer.

    • Like an answer.

    • See the number of questions, answers, comments and likes.

Note

  • In Community Checking, the All Questions area appears in the Navigation pane when there are questions in two or more books. There you see all the questions, not limited to a specific book.

  • Questions that have not been read have a bold font.

  • The number of current answers to each question appears near each question. Example:

  • (question mark) appears in the text of the book that has a question and that text is highlighted.

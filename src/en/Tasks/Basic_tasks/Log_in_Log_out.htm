Log in / Log out

Log in

  • After you have signed up, you can do any of these steps to Log In again:

    • Click LOG IN WITH PARATEXT, and then follow the steps in the Authorise Application pages.

Your role in the Paratext project is the same in Scripture Forge if you used LOG IN WITH PARATEXT at least one time.

After that, your project role is remembered if you log in with the other log in options below.

  • Click LOG IN WITH GOOGLE, and then follow the steps in the Sign in with Google pages.

  • Click LOG IN WITH FACEBOOK, and then follow the steps in the Log Into Facebook pages.

  • Click LOG IN WITH PHONE NUMBER.

  • If the country code you see is correct, type your phone number.

  • Otherwise, click the arrow () and search for your country code.  Then, type your phone number.

A code is sent to your phone.

Type that code to log in. 

  • Type your email address and your password in the boxes, and then click LOG IN.

Log out

  • To log out, on the Top App Bar, click your avatar and then click Log out.

Important

  • All Scripture Forge data is removed from your computer or device each time you log out. So, if you want to save changes you made while you were offline () do not log out until you have an internet connection ().
    Changes you make while offline are stored locally and will be sent to the server the next time you have an internet connection. However, that local storage is cleared anytime you log out. Logging out does not remove data from the server.

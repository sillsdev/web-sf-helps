Read or hear an answer

People with the role of Administrator can read or hear answers from others. People in other roles can only read or hear answers after they add an answer themselves.

  1. In the Navigation pane, click Community Checking.

  2. Click the book ( <name>) that has the answered questions.

The Questions pane appears next to the pane with the book's text.

  1. Click the question you want to see.

A pane appears below the book text. It displays the question and below that you see any comments and answers.

If necessary, click and drag the divider () between the panes to resize them or use the scroll bar.

  1. Here are things you can do, depending on your role and whether you wrote the question, answer or comments:

    • Read an answer that was typed.

    • Click the play button () to hear an answer that was recorded.

    • Click ADD A COMMENT to add a comment.

    • Click EDIT to edit a question or comment.

    • Click DELETE to delete a question, answer or comment.

    • Click to like an answer.

    • Click PREVIOUS or NEXT to see other questions.

Note

  • If you see an Audio unavailable message, click the question mark button () to learn why.

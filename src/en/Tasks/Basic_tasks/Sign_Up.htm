Sign Up

  1. In a web browser, go to https://scriptureforge.org/.

Or, if you received an email invitation to join a project, click the project link URL in that email.

  1. Click Sign Up.

Do one of these steps:

    • Click SIGN UP WITH PARATEXT.

For people with a Paratext account, this is the recommended way to sign up.

If you have not registered for Paratext, you can click here to register for free.

People with the role of Administrator must have a Paratext account.

    • Click SIGN UP WITH GOOGLE.

    • Click SIGN UP WITH FACEBOOK, and then follow the steps in the Log Into Facebook pages.

    • Click LOG IN WITH PHONE NUMBER.

  • If the country code you see is correct, type your phone number.

  • Otherwise, click the arrow () and search for your country code.  Then, type your phone number.

A code is sent to your phone.

Type that code to log in. 

  • Type your email address and your password in the boxes. Click SIGN UP.

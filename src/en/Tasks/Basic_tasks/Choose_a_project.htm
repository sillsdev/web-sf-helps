Choose a project

  1. On the Top App Bar, click your avatar, and then click Project home.

The current project, if any is displayed at the top of the Navigation pane.

  1. To choose a different project, click the down arrow () and then click a project from the list.

Now you can click Translate or Community Checking to choose a book or use other commands (depending on role).

If the project you want is not connected, contact your project's administrator.

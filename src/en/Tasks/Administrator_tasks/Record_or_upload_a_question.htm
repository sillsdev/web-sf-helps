Record or upload a question

  • Do the steps in Add a question until the New Question dialog box is open and you have chosen the Scripture reference, End reference.

Then, do one of the options shown below.

Upload an existing audio file

  1. Click UPLOAD AUDIO FILE.

  2. Navigate to the file that contains your answer, click it and then click Open.

  3. Click play () and hear that audio file.

  4. If you do not want to use that audio file, click REMOVE AUDIO FILE. Otherwise, click SAVE.

Record an answer

  1. Connect a microphone to your computer.

  2. Click RECORD and then speak your answer into your microphone.

  3. Click STOP RECORDING.

  4. Click play () to hear your recorded answer.

  5. If you do not like it, click TRY AGAIN and then record another answer. Otherwise, click SAVE.

Note

  • If you have no Internet access, you can add questions even if they include audio. The audio is stored on your device so your questions are available while you have no Internet access.

Later, when you have access to the Internet, the questions and audio files are uploaded to the Scripture Forge server. After that, you can listen to other people's question and answers.

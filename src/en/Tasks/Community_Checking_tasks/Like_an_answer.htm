Like an answer

  1. With answers to a question in view, do one of these steps:

    • Click to indicate that you like an answer.

The number below the button increases by one.

    • Click again to not like an answer.

The number below the button decreases by one.

Note

  • Administrators cannot like questions but can see how many likes or dislikes each answer received.

  • People in other roles can only like other people's answers, not their own.

  • You cannot see an archived question or its answers or comments.

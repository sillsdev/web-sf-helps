Record or upload an answer

  • Do the steps in Add an answer (or Edit an answer) until you have clicked RECORD/UPLOAD.

Then, do one of the options shown below.

Upload an existing audio file

  1. Click UPLOAD AUDIO FILE.

  2. Navigate to the file that contains your answer, click it and then click Open.

  3. Click play () and hear that audio file.

  4. If you do not want to use that audio file, click REMOVE AUDIO FILE. Otherwise, click SAVE ANSWER.

Record an answer

  1. Connect a microphone to your computer.

  2. Click RECORD and then speak your answer into your microphone.

  3. Click STOP RECORDING.

  4. Click play () to hear your recorded answer.

  5. If you do not like it, click TRY AGAIN and then record another answer. Otherwise, click SAVE ANSWER.

Note

  • If you have no Internet access, you can add answers even if they include audio.

Later, when you have access to the Internet, the answers and audio files are uploaded to the Scripture Forge server.

If you see an Audio unavailable message, click the question mark button () to learn why.

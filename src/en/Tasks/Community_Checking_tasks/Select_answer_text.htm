Select answer text

  • Do the steps in Add an answer until you have clicked SELECT TEXT.

Then, do these steps.

  1. Click SELECT VERSES.

The Select scripture to attach to your answer dialog box appears. It displays the current chapter of the book you are working in. But, you can change the book or chapter.

  1. If you want to change the book or chapter, click the down arrow () that appears near the book name.

Then, do these steps:

    • In the Choose book dialog box, click the book that has the text you want.

    • In the Choose chapter dialog box, click the chapter that has the text you want.

    • The Select scripture to attach to your answer dialog box appears again and displays the book and chapter.

  1. Highlight words in either of these ways:

    • With your mouse, click and drag your mouse pointer over the text you want.

    • Click a word and then press and hold the Shift key while you use the left or right arrow key to highlight more words.

  2. Click SAVE.

  3. Click SAVE ANSWER.

Community Checking tasks overview

Here are Community Checking tasks. Depending on what is enabled, people with the role of Community Checker (Checker) or Reviewer can do them.

  • Add a comment

  • Add an answer

    • Record or upload an answer

  • Delete an answer or comment

  • Edit an answer or comment

  • Like an answer

  • Select answer text

Tip

  • If there are questions in more than one book, All Questions is available in the Navigation pane.

Click it to see questions in other books.

  • A checker or reviewer (user) must have Internet access to join a project. Once a user is in the project and after the first time that user has gone to Community Checking, the text and audio for questions will be available whether online or offline (no Internet connection). The question audio files are downloaded to the user's computer or device so they can be played even without an Internet connection.

An Internet connection is required to receive text and audio information added after that by others, such as other people's answers. A warning message appears if you do not have an Internet connection and you try to play an audio file that is not saved on your computer or device.

  • If you see an Audio unavailable message, click the question mark button () to learn why.

Delete an answer or comment

People with the role of project Administrator can delete their own answers or comments and also those written by other people. See Also: Work with answers.

People in other roles can only delete one of their own answers or comments.

  • Click DELETE, which is below the particular answer or comment you no longer want to keep.

The answer or comment is deleted.

Note

  • You cannot see an archived question or its answers or comments.

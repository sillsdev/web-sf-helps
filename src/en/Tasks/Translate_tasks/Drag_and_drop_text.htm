Drag and drop text

You can drag and drop text into Scripture Forge while you are translating verses in a chapter.

  1. Size and position both source and Scripture Forge so they do not overlap each other on your screen.

  2. Do these steps:

    • In the source, select the text you want.

    • Left-click the selected (highlighted) text and keep the mouse button held down while you move the pointer to the verse container in Scripture Forge.

    • Release your mouse button to drop that text there.

Note

  • The drag and drop process does not preserve line breaks or text formatting, such as underlined, bold, colored or strike through.

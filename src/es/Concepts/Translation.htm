Translation

People with the role of Translator translate textual scripture contents from one language to another. Typically, a based-on translation is used as the source project.

Community Checking, questions and comments help to refine the translation.

One of the ways translation progress is measured is count how many segments have any text, with no regard to completeness or quality. Administrators can see these numbers when they review translation progress.

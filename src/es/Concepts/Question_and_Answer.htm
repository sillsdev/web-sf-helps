Pregunta y Respuesta

Una pregunta es una consulta que pretende obtener información o aclaraciones de otras personas.

Una respuesta es una contestación a la pregunta. Ver también: Comentarios.

  • Personas con el rol de Administrador, y posiblemente otros con permiso, pueden hacer todas o algunas de estas tareas:

    • Añadir preguntas para dirigir y controlar las discusiones sobre el trabajo de traducción. Las preguntas pueden ser escritas en texto y/o grabadas en audio.

Admins can see all answers to a question without having to answer that question themselves. 

  • Archive or republish a question.

  • Delete a question.

  • Delete another person's answer.

  • Edit their questions and their own answers.

  • Import questions from Transcelerator.

  • Read or hear an answer.

  • Work with answers (export, resolve or delete).

  • See the counts and icons of questions, answers, comments and likes.

  • People with other roles can do these tasks, if they are enabled:

    • Add an answer. Answers can be typed text and/or recorded audio.

Other peoples' answers are hidden until you add your own answer. This way your answer is not affected by other existing answers. If you see a link that says Show 1 or more answers (or similar), click it to see other answers.

  • Add a comment. 

  • Delete your answer.

  • Edit your answer.

  • Like an answer.

  • See the number of questions, answers, comments and likes.

Nota

  • On the Users page, permissions can be granted or denied regarding who can add & edit questions.

  • In Community Checking, the All Questions area appears in the Navigation pane when there are questions in two or more books. There you see all the questions, not limited to a specific book.

  • Questions that have not been read have a bold font.

  • The number of current answers to each question appears near each question. Example:

  • (question mark) appears in the text of the book that has a question and that text is highlighted.

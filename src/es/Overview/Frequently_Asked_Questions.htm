Who can... ?

  • Roles lists the roles used in Scripture Forge. Tasks overview lists tasks by role.

How do I... ?

  • How do I change roles? Paratext roles can only be changed in Paratext. If others were invited to check a translation, they are given the role of Checker.

Can I... ?

  • Can I ask a question? Only people with the role of Administrator can add a question. People in other roles can add an answer or add a comment.

Why can't I... ?

  • Why can't I see a question? You cannot see an archived question or its answers or comments.

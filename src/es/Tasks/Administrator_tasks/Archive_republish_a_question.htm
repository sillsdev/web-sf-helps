Archive or republish questions

People with role of Administrator can archive a question and later choose to republish it. Community Checking describes the controls.

Archive

  1. In the Navigation pane, click Community Checking.

  2. Do one of these steps:

    • Click the book () that has the question you want to archive. In the Questions column, click the question. Then, below the book's text, click ARCHIVE.

    • Click Overview. In the Texts with Questions pane. Click the arrow () for a book to see the chapters with questions. Click the arrow () for a chapter to see the questions in that chapter. Click the archive button () that appears next to the question you want to archive.

The question and any answers and comments are no longer available to be read by other users. In Overview, the question moves from the Texts with Questions pane to the Archived Questions pane.

Republish

  1. In the Navigation pane, click Community Checking.

  2. Click Overview.

The Archived Questions pane appears. Books that have archived questions appear below it.

  1. Click the arrow () for a book to see the chapters with questions.

  2. Click the arrow () for a chapter to see the questions in that chapter.

  3. Click the republish button () that appears next to the question you do not want in the archive.

That question moves from the Archived Questions pane back to the Texts with Questions pane.
